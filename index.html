Why New York?

New York, constituent state of USA

New York, often called New York City or simply NYC, is the most populous city in the United States, located at the southern tip of New York State on one of the world's largest natural harbors. The city comprises five boroughs, each of which is coextensive with a respective county.

New York is a center for worldwide banking and finance, health care and life sciences, medical technology and research, retailing, world trade, transportation, tourism, real estate, new media, traditional media, advertising, legal services, accountancy, insurance, and the arts in the United States; while Silicon Alley, metonymous for New York's broad-spectrum high technology sphere, continues to expand.

Da Andrea - Greenwich Village

Da Andrea Restaurant is a locally-owned and operated, Northern Italian restaurant in the heart of West Village, New York City that has been around for more than two decades. Da Andrea offers traditional Italian cuisine, regional specialties, seasonal dishes, an extensive wine selection and craft cocktails for our guests to enjoy.

Address:

New York, NY 10011 Avenue Of The Americas & 5th Ave Greenwich Village

What I like about it
